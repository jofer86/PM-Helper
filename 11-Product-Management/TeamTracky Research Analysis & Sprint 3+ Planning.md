## ðŸ”„ Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
1. **Payment Integration Complexity**
   - **Risk**: Mexican payment processors integration challenges
   - **Mitigation**: Start with Stripe Mexico, add Conekta as secondary

2. **Document Storage Security**
   - **Risk**: Sensitive document handling and privacy
   - **Mitigation**: Implement encryption, secure cloud storage

3. **Multi-Team Performance**
   - **Risk**: Database performance with complex team relationships
   - **Mitigation**: Optimize queries, implement caching

### Market Risks
1. **Feature Parity Pressure**
   - **Risk**: Trying to match all TeamTracky features immediately
   - **Mitigation**: Focus on Mexican market differentiators first

2. **Development Velocity**
   - **Risk**: Adding complexity may slow Sprint velocity
   - **Mitigation**: Maintain 16-18 point sprints, defer nice-to-have features
