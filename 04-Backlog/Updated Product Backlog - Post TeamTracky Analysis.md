## ðŸŽ¯ Strategic Priority Matrix (Updated)

### Critical Path Features (Must Have for Market Entry)
1. **Payment Integration** - Revenue enablement and professional credibility
2. **Document Management** - Legal compliance and liability protection  
3. **Multi-Team Support** - Coach adoption and retention
4. **Bucket Roster System** - Unique value proposition for tryouts
5. **Calendar Integration** - Parent adoption driver

### Competitive Differentiators (Mexican Market Advantage)
1. **WhatsApp Integration** - More relevant than live chat in Mexico
2. **OXXO Payment Support** - Local payment preference
3. **Spanish-First UX** - Better than translated interfaces
4. **Mexican Sports Culture** - Local holidays, leagues, customs
5. **Peso-First Pricing** - Local currency and pricing strategies

### Nice-to-Have Features (Future Consideration)
1. **Advanced Analytics** - Professional insights and reporting
2. **Tournament Management** - League-level functionality
3. **Equipment Tracking** - Inventory management
4. **Multi-Sport Support** - Platform expansion
5. **API Integrations** - Third-party ecosystem
